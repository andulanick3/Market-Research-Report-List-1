<p><p>The seamless pipes market is primarily categorized into hot-rolled and cold-rolled types, each suited for different applications. Hot-rolled seamless pipes are produced at high temperatures, making them ideal for structural applications due to their higher strength and ductility. In contrast, cold-rolled seamless pipes undergo processing at room temperature, resulting in enhanced precision, surface finish, and dimensional accuracy, making them suitable for applications requiring tight tolerances. Both types serve critical roles in construction, oil and gas, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
