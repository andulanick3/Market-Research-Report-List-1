<p><p>The Medium Chain Triglycerides (MCT) market is divided into two main types: oil and powder. Oil MCTs are liquid fats derived from coconut oil or palm kernel oil, commonly used in cooking, dietary supplements, and food products for their quick energy source and potential health benefits. Powder MCTs, on the other hand, are MCTs that have been converted into a powdered form, making them easier to incorporate into various products like protein powders, beverages, and meal replacements, enhancing their versatility and appeal.</p></p>
