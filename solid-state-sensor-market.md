<p><p>The solid-state sensor market encompasses various types, including phased array sensors and microelectromechanical systems (MEMS). Phased array sensors utilize multiple antennas to detect and analyze signals from different angles, enhancing precision and speed for applications like radar and telecommunications. On the other hand, MEMS sensors are miniaturized devices that integrate mechanical and electrical components on a single chip, allowing for high sensitivity and versatility in applications such as automotive, healthcare, and consumer electronics. Both types drive innovation in sensor technology.</p></p>
