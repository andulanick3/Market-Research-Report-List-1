<p><ul><li>Single Pump Hemodialysis Machines</li><li>Double Pump Hemodialysis Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hemodialysis machines market is primarily categorized into single pump and double pump machines. Single pump hemodialysis machines utilize one pump to circulate blood and dialysate, making them simpler and more cost-effective for basic dialysis procedures. In contrast, double pump machines employ two pumps, enhancing efficiency and allowing simultaneous blood and dialysate flow, which can improve treatment outcomes. The choice between these types depends on patient needs, healthcare facility capabilities, and desired treatment complexity.</p></p>
