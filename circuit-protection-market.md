<p><strong>The Circuit Protection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Overcurrent Protection</li><li>ESD Protection</li><li>Overvoltage Protection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The circuit protection market encompasses various types designed to safeguard electronic devices. Overcurrent protection prevents excessive current flow, using devices like fuses and circuit breakers to avoid component damage. ESD protection shields against electrostatic discharge, which can harm sensitive electronics, employing components such as TVS diodes and varistors. Overvoltage protection defends circuits from voltage spikes, utilizing surge protectors and transient voltage suppressors to maintain safe operational levels. Together, these solutions ensure reliability and longevity in electronic systems.</p></p>
