<p><p>Thermopile detectors are sensors that measure temperature variations and can be categorized into single-channel and multi-channel types. Single-channel detectors are designed to detect thermal radiation from a single source, often used in applications like non-contact temperature measurement. In contrast, multi-channel detectors incorporate multiple sensing elements, allowing simultaneous detection of temperatures from various sources, enhancing precision and data collection. Both types find applications in diverse fields, including industrial monitoring, medical diagnostics, and environmental sensing, catering to varying measurement needs.</p></p>
