<p><strong>The GaN FET Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Depletion Mode</li><li>Enhancement Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GaN FET market is segmented into depletion mode and enhancement mode devices. Depletion mode GaN FETs are normally-on transistors that conduct in their default state, offering high efficiency and fast switching capabilities, making them suitable for power applications. In contrast, enhancement mode GaN FETs are normally-off, requiring a positive gate voltage to conduct, which enhances safety and simplifies circuit design. Both types cater to diverse applications, including renewable energy systems, electric vehicles, and advanced power management.</p></p>
