<p><p>The Natural Quartz Sand Market can be categorized into two main production types: Dry Process Production and Water Production. Dry Process Production involves grinding and separating quartz sand through mechanical means without the use of water, leading to a cleaner, more consistent product suitable for applications like glass manufacturing and construction. In contrast, Water Production utilizes water to help in the extraction and washing of quartz, resulting in finely graded sand ideal for industrial uses such as filtration and concrete production.</p></p>
