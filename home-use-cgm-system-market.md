<p><p>The Home Use Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) market includes two main types: Retrospective CGM and Real-Time Monitoring. Retrospective CGM systems collect glucose data over time for users to review later, aiding in trend analysis and management decisions. In contrast, Real-Time Monitoring systems provide instantaneous glucose readings and alerts, enabling immediate responses to fluctuations. Both types cater to individuals with diabetes, enhancing self-management through different approaches to glucose tracking and data utilization.</p></p>
