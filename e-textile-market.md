<p><strong>The E-textile Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Electronic Textiles</li><li>Active Electronic Textiles</li><li>Ultra-Electronic Textiles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The e-textile market is categorized into three main types. Passive electronic textiles incorporate conductive threads and fabrics that serve basic functionalities like sensing without requiring external power sources. Active electronic textiles, in contrast, integrate power sources, enabling dynamic features such as moisture management or temperature control. Ultra-electronic textiles push the boundaries further, embedding advanced electronics, sensors, and communication devices directly into the fabric, facilitating complex interactions such as real-time health monitoring or data transmission in wearable technology.</p></p>
