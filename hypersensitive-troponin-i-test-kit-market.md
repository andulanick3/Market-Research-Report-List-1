TRIF utilizes fluorescent labels to detect Troponin I levels, providing rapid and sensitive results through spatial resolution. CLIA employs chemiluminescent reactions for the quantification of Troponin I, known for its high sensitivity and accuracy. Both methods are pivotal in diagnosing myocardial infarctions, enhancing patient management by allowing for early detection of cardiac issues.</p></p>
