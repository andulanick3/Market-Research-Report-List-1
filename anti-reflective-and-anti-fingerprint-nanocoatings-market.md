<p><p>The anti-reflective and anti-fingerprint nanocoatings market is driven by advancements in coating technologies, including resistance heating and electron beam heating methods. Resistance heating utilizes electrical resistance to rapidly heat substrates, ensuring even coating application for enhanced durability and functionality. In contrast, electron beam heating employs high-energy electrons to precisely cure and bond coatings at a molecular level, resulting in superior transparency and scratch resistance. Both methods significantly enhance the performance and lifespan of coatings in various applications, including electronics and optics.</p></p>
