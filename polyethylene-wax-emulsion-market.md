<p><p>The Polyethylene Wax Emulsion market is categorized based on the ionic nature of the emulsions. Cationic emulsions carry a positive charge, making them suitable for applications requiring strong adhesion and compatibility with negatively charged surfaces. Anionic emulsions, characterized by a negative charge, provide excellent dispersibility and stability in water, making them ideal for coatings and inks. Non-ionic emulsions, which possess no charge, offer versatile compatibility with various substrates and are used in applications where ionic stability is essential.</p></p>
