<p><p>The metal valves and pipe fittings market can be categorized into four main types based on actuation methods: manual, pneumatic, hydraulic, and electric. Manual valves are operated by hand, providing simplicity and reliability. Pneumatic valves utilize compressed air for control, suitable for rapid and precise operations. Hydraulic valves leverage liquid pressure for more robust applications, ideal in heavy machinery. Electric valves use electrical signals for automation, offering enhanced control and integration in complex systems. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
