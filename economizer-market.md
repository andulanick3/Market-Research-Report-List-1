<p><ul><li>Fluid Economizers</li><li>Air-side Economizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The economizer market encompasses two primary types: fluid economizers and air-side economizers. Fluid economizers utilize water or other fluids to enhance heat exchange efficiency in HVAC systems, lowering energy consumption and operational costs. In contrast, air-side economizers leverage outside air to cool indoor spaces, reducing reliance on mechanical cooling. Both types contribute to improved energy efficiency and sustainability in commercial and industrial buildings, enabling significant cost savings and reduced environmental impact through optimized energy usage.</p></p>
