<p><strong>The Structural Adhesive Tape Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Sided Bonding</li><li>Double Sided Bonding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The structural adhesive tape market is primarily categorized into two types: single-sided bonding and double-sided bonding. Single-sided bonding tapes feature adhesive on one side, ideal for applications requiring attachment to a single surface, such as mounting and securing components. In contrast, double-sided bonding tapes have adhesive on both sides, providing strong adhesion between two surfaces, making them suitable for applications like laminating, bonding, and crafting. Both types offer versatility and durability across various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
