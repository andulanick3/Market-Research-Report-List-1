<p><ul><li>Open(Open-Circuit)the Respiratory System</li><li>Enclosed(Closed-Circuit) the Respiratory System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The freediving respiratory systems market primarily consists of open and enclosed circuit systems. Open-circuit systems release exhaled air into the surrounding environment, allowing for straightforward breathing during dives. These systems are popular for recreational freediving due to their simplicity and ease of use. In contrast, enclosed or closed-circuit systems recycle exhaled air, providing enhanced gas efficiency and longer dive times. They are typically utilized for deeper and more advanced dives, catering to experienced divers seeking prolonged underwater exploration.</p></p>
