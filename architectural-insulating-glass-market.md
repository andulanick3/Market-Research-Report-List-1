<p><p>Architectural Insulating Glass (AIG) refers to multi-layered glass units that provide superior thermal insulation, helping to enhance energy efficiency in buildings. The rising demand for energy-efficient building materials and growing environmental concerns are driving the expansion of the AIG market.</p><p>The architectural insulating glass market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14% during the forecast period. Low-E insulating glass units have a special coating that reflects infrared light, further improving thermal performance. Each type serves unique energy-saving and aesthetic needs in building design.</p></p>
