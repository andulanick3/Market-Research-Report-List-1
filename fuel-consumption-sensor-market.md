Pointer dashboards utilize analog gauges with needles to indicate fuel consumption levels, providing a traditional visual representation. In contrast, digital display instrument panels employ electronic screens to present detailed and precise information about fuel usage, often with additional functionalities like trip data and real-time analytics. Both types cater to different consumer preferences for monitoring vehicle efficiency and enhancing driving experiences.</p></p>
