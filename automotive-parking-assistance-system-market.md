<p><p>The Automotive Parking Assistance System market is primarily segmented into two types: camera-based systems and ultrasonic sensor systems. Camera-based systems utilize visual data to provide drivers with real-time images and overlays, enhancing awareness of surrounding obstacles. These systems often include features like 360-degree views and object detection. On the other hand, ultrasonic sensor systems use sound waves to detect nearby objects and gauge distances, alerting drivers with audio or visual signals. Both technologies improve safety and ease of parking.</p></p>
