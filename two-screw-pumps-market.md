<p><p>The two-screw pump market is primarily categorized into double-suction and single-suction twin-screw pumps. Double-suction twin-screw pumps feature two inlets, allowing for higher flow rates and enhanced efficiency, making them suitable for large-scale applications. In contrast, single-suction twin-screw pumps have one inlet, typically used for lower flow rates and smaller systems. Both types offer benefits such as reliable performance, reduced pulsation, and versatility in handling various fluids, making them essential in industries like oil and gas, chemicals, and water management.</p></p>
