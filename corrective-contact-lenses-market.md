<p><strong>The Corrective Contact Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Soft Contact Lenses</li><li>Rigid Gas Permeable (RGP) Contact Lenses</li><li>Hybrid Contact Lenses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The corrective contact lenses market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Soft Contact Lenses** are made from flexible materials, providing comfort and easier adaptation for users. </p><p>2. **Rigid Gas Permeable (RGP) Contact Lenses** offer excellent vision correction and durability, allowing oxygen to pass through while maintaining shape. </p><p>3. **Hybrid Contact Lenses** combine the benefits of both soft and RGP lenses, featuring a rigid center for vision clarity and a soft outer ring for comfort. This variety caters to diverse vision needs and preferences.</p></p>
