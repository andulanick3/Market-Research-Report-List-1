<p><p>The dental operating lights market comprises two primary types: fixed and mobile lights. Fixed dental lights are permanently installed in dental offices, providing consistent illumination during procedures. They are typically mounted on the ceiling or wall, offering stability and optimal positioning. In contrast, mobile dental lights are portable and can be easily moved between treatment areas, making them ideal for clinics with limited space or for use in mobile dentistry. Both types are essential for ensuring precision and visibility during dental procedures.</p></p>
