<p><strong>The Affiliate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cost Per Sale</li><li>Cost Per Lead</li><li>Cost Per Click</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Affiliate marketing encompasses various compensation structures, including Cost Per Sale (CPS), Cost Per Lead (CPL), and Cost Per Click (CPC). CPS rewards affiliates based on actual sales generated through their referral links, making it a performance-driven model. CPL pays affiliates for each lead or potential customer they deliver, focusing on interests rather than immediate sales. In contrast, CPC compensates affiliates for every click generated on ads, prioritizing traffic over conversions. Each model serves different marketing goals and strategies.</p></p>
