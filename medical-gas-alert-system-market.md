<p><p>The Medical Gas Alert System market encompasses three primary types: Area Alarms, Master Alarms, and Combination Alarms. Area Alarms are designed to monitor specific zones, providing localized alerts for gas malfunctions. Master Alarms consolidate information from multiple Area Alarms, offering a central point of monitoring for an entire facility or department. Combination Alarms integrate both functions, delivering comprehensive monitoring and alerting capabilities. These systems ensure patient safety by promptly notifying medical staff of any gas supply issues.</p></p>
