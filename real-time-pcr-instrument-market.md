<p><strong>The Real-time PCR Instrument Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-step Real Time PCR</li><li>Two-step Real Time PCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Real-time PCR instrument market is categorized into two main types: one-step and two-step Real-time PCR. One-step Real-time PCR consolidates the reverse transcription and amplification processes into a single reaction, simplifying workflows and minimizing contamination risk. Conversely, two-step Real-time PCR separates these stages, allowing for more flexibility and optimized conditions for each process. Both types serve diverse applications in research, clinical diagnostics, and biotechnology, catering to varying specific needs in nucleic acid quantification and analysis.</p></p>
