<p><p>The Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) drugs market includes several key types of medications. ACE inhibitors help relax blood vessels and lower blood pressure, reducing kidney strain. Angiotensin-II receptor blockers (ARBs) similarly manage hypertension while protecting renal function. Calcium channel blockers decrease heart strain and improve blood flow, which can benefit kidney health. Beta blockers, such as Brevibloc and Bystolic, manage heart rate and blood pressure, supporting overall cardiovascular function, crucial for patients with CKD. Additional drugs within these categories also contribute to effective management of CKD complications.</p></p>
