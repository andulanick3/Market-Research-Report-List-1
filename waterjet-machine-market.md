<p><ul><li>High Pressure</li><li>Low Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waterjet machine market is segmented into high-pressure and low-pressure systems, each serving distinct applications. High-pressure waterjet machines operate at pressures exceeding 30,000 PSI, ideal for cutting, carving, and abrasive applications in materials like metal and stone, offering precision and versatility. In contrast, low-pressure systems, typically below 30,000 PSI, are suitable for cleaning and surface preparation tasks, focusing on applications where less force is needed. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, enhancing efficiency in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
