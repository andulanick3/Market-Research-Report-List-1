<p><p>The Silicon Photonics Transceiver market encompasses three primary components: lasers, photodetectors, and modulators. Lasers serve as the light source for data transmission, employing integrated photonic devices to achieve high-speed communication. Photodetectors convert incoming optical signals into electrical signals, ensuring data reception accuracy. Modulators control light signals to encode information for transmission, facilitating efficient data transfer. Together, these components enable the development of high-performance, energy-efficient optical communication systems, vital for data centers and telecommunications.</p></p>
