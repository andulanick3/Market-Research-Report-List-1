<p><p>The metalworking lubricants market encompasses various types that enhance machining efficiency and protect materials. Quenching lubricants are utilized during heat treatment processes to cool and harden metals, ensuring optimal properties. Cutting lubricants reduce friction and heat in machining operations, extending tool life and improving surface finish. Grinding lubricants facilitate the grinding process by minimizing wear and resisting rust, while corrosion preventives protect metal surfaces from oxidation and moisture, ensuring longevity and reliability of metal parts in various applications.</p></p>
